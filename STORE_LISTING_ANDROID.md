## ğŸ” Google Play Review Tips

### Common Rejection Reasons

**1. Cannabis Content**
- **Issue:** Google may flag drug-related apps
- **Solution:**
  - Emphasize health/wellness tracking aspect
  - Clear 17+ age rating
  - Only distribute in legal jurisdictions
  - Disclaimer: "Does not facilitate sales"
  - Position as "harm reduction" tool

**2. Privacy Policy Missing**
- **Issue:** Privacy policy not accessible
- **Solution:**
  - Host privacy policy at public URL
  - Link in app listing
  - Also accessible in-app

**3. Misleading Content**
- **Issue:** Screenshots show features that don't work
- **Solution:**
  - Only screenshot working features
  - Remove "Herb Pro" references from screenshots
  - Mark premium features clearly as "Coming Soon"

**4. Inappropriate Content Rating**
- **Issue:** Content rating doesn't match app content
- **Solution:**
  - Answer questionnaire honestly
  - Select "Mature 17+" rating
  - Acknowledge drug references

**5. Target SDK Version**
- **Issue:** App targets old Android SDK
- **Solution:**
  - Target SDK 34 (Android 14) minimum
  - Already configured in app.json
